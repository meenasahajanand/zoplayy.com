DESCRIPTION

Soundmap: Find Your Songs is a unique and creative music exploration game that takes you into a musical world full of melodies, rhythms and colors. In the game, players will take on the role of a music explorer and embark on a fantastic journey to find lost sheet music. By unlocking a music puzzle, discover the story hidden behind the melody, and feel the endless charm of music to us.
Soundmap: Find Your Songs builds a music world full of fantasy colors, where there are forests, deserts, oceans and other terrains, and each terrain has its own unique music style. During the exploration, players will unlock the music of different regions and experience the music culture around the world.
In the game, players will follow the footsteps of the main character to unveil the mystery of the music world. In the process, players will meet various NPCs, interact with them, learn their stories, and gradually reveal the mystery of the whole music world.
Soundmap: Find Your Songs features a variety of music styles, including classical, pop, rock, folk, and more. Players can enjoy these music styles in the game and improve their music appreciation skills. In addition to the adventure mode, the game also features an arena where players can compete with other players and show off their musical talents. Players will explore different musical areas, including forests, oceans, cities and mountains, etc. Each area has a unique visual style and musical theme. Each place is rich in scenes and hidden corners, players need to look carefully to find objects and characters that can be interacted with.
Players can interact with objects in the environment, for example by playing instruments, touching specific notes, and unlocking musical puzzles.
These interactions not only reveal the story of the game, but also activate the hidden music. The core gameplay in the game is musical puzzles. Each area has unique puzzles that need to be solved musically. Players need to find specific combinations of notes, play instruments or activate certain organs through sound waves.
During exploration, players can collect notes scattered throughout the area, each with its own unique sound and properties.
Players can use these notes to unlock new areas or upgrade their abilities.
Rhythm challenges are also set in some areas, where players need to tap or swipe according to the rhythm of the music in order to complete the task. Upon successful challenges, players will be rewarded with great prizes and new melodies.
In Soundmap: Find Your Songs, players will encounter several interesting musical characters. Each character has its own story and mission. By completing the character's quests, players can delve into the history of the Music Realm and gain unique props or skills.
When conversing with a character, players can choose different dialog options that affect the development and ending of the story.
Certain characters will also provide musical clues to help players find lost melodies.
Players can also interact with other online players, form squads to collaborate on certain difficult musical tasks, or compete against others in specific tournaments to showcase their musical talents.
The game has a built-in music composition tool that allows players to create their own melodies based on collected notes and elements. Players can use these tools to create personalized musical compositions and share them with other players.
Players can showcase their creations in the community and interact with other music lovers for feedback and inspiration.
This not only adds to the social aspect of the game, but also encourages players to be creative and explore the infinite possibilities of music.
The game features a variety of quests and achievement systems that allow players to unlock rewards, titles, and brand new content after completing specific tasks. Tasks include collecting specific notes, unlocking new areas, completing musical challenges, and more, adding to the game's playability. The in-game achievement system encourages players to explore every corner and discover hidden content. Completing achievements not only improves the player's skills, but also allows the player to gain recognition in the community.
The visual style of Soundmap: Find Your Songs is full of dreamy colors, and the design of each area echoes its musical theme. The lighting effects and colors are just right, allowing the player to feel the atmosphere and emotion of the music.
The sound effects in the game are subtle and detailed, from the ambient sounds to the character soundtracks, all of which have been carefully designed to ensure that the player is immersed in the world of music.
Each note resonates with the player's emotions, making every step of the quest fun.

DESCRIPTION

Black Myth: Wukong is an action role-playing game created by Game Science. The game is based on the Monkey King from the Chinese classic Journey to the West, and is built on Unreal Engine 4, creating a fantasy world full of mythological colors and oriental aesthetics. The following is a brief introduction to the game, including gameplay.
The story of Black Myth: Wukong is based on the Journey to the West, which tells the story of the Monkey King, who was born from a rock and went through trials and tribulations to become the Buddha. In the game, players will take on the role of the Monkey King, experiencing his growth process, exploring the mysterious world, and revealing the truth hidden behind the myth.
The combat system of Black Myth: Wukong is a blend of traditional martial arts, fairy arts, and demonic skills. Players can combine different moves to form a unique fighting style. In the game, the Monkey King has a variety of combat forms, such as the Monkey King form and the Fighting Buddha form, each of which has unique skills and combat characteristics.
The combat system mainly includes the following aspects:
(1) Normal Attacks: Players can launch consecutive normal attacks to inflict damage on enemies.
Skill Attacks: In the game, the Monkey King possesses a variety of skills, including the Golden Hoop Stick, Somersault Cloud, and Seventy-two Changes. Players can unleash powerful skills to attack enemies by consuming chi.
Transformation System: The Monkey King can transform into various monsters to gain corresponding abilities. For example, he can transform into a bear monster to improve his defense, and into a Peng Demon King to improve his flying ability.
Combo System: Players can combine normal attacks, skill attacks and transformation skills to form a combo to inflict higher damage on the enemy.
Black Myth: Wukong has a huge open world that players can freely explore in the game. The game world is divided into several areas, including the Mountain of Flowers and Fruits, the Heavenly Court, and the Earthly Palace. Each area has unique terrain, monsters and quests.
The exploration system mainly includes the following aspects:
Mainline Quests: In the game, players need to complete a series of mainline quests to advance the story. The main quests will guide players to explore various areas and unlock new skills and storylines.
(2) Spur quests: In the game, players can take various spur quests to understand the background of the worldview and enrich the game experience.
Hidden Quests: There are many secrets hidden in the game world waiting to be discovered by the player. Completing the hidden quests, players can obtain rare props and skills.
Black Myth: Wukong has a rich equipment system where players can upgrade, build and synthesize to get more powerful equipment.
The equipment system mainly includes the following aspects:
Weapon: In the game, the Monkey King can use a variety of weapons, such as the Golden Rod, Lance, and Twin Swords. Different weapons have different attack methods and skills.
Defenses: There are various kinds of defenses in the game, including head, body, hands, feet and other parts. Equipping defensive gears can enhance the Monkey King's defense, life value and other attributes.
Accessories: In the game, accessories can enhance various attributes of the Monkey King, such as attack power, defense power, speed and so on.
In Black Myth: Wukong, the Monkey King can improve his strength through cultivation. The cultivation system includes the following aspects:
(1) Internal Power: Cultivating internal power can improve the Monkey King's chi value, life value and attack power.
(2) External Power: Cultivating External Power can increase the damage of Sun Wukong's moves and the effects of his combos.
Immortal Arts: Learning Immortal Arts allows the Monkey King to master more powerful skills.
Black Myth: Wukong is an action-packed role-playing game filled with oriental fantasy in which players will take on the role of the Monkey King and experience a thrilling mythological adventure. Gameplay is rich and varied, including combat, exploration, equipment and cultivation systems, providing players with an immersive gaming experience.

DESCRIPTION

Alice Wonder Match is a casual game that combines classic three-canceling gameplay with fairy tale adventure elements. With the fantastic Alice Wonderland as the background, the game incorporates a rich fantasy story and diverse character experiences. Players will explore a world full of magic and mystery, help Alice, the protagonist, to solve the puzzles, repair the broken Wonderland, and unveil the secrets hidden in the depths of the Wonderland. The game brings players a unique fairy tale adventure with its diverse gameplay design, profound plot content and exquisite graphics.
The main gameplay of the game is centered on the triple elimination mechanism, where players need to slide elements on the board to connect elements of the same color or pattern into a line to complete elimination. When three or more identical elements are connected, they will disappear and the player will gain points or resources. Meanwhile, each level sets different objectives, such as collecting a specific number of certain elements, clearing obstacles, or completing a task within a limited number of steps. As the game progresses, the difficulty of the levels will gradually increase, with more constraints and challenging designs, such as frozen cubes, immovable obstacles, and special bricks that need to be broken multiple times, which require players to be more flexible in planning their strategies and utilizing their limited resources.
On top of the three-canceling gameplay, the game introduces a rich skill and prop system. Players can generate special elements through specific elimination methods, such as bombs, straight line eliminators and rainbow balls, etc. These special elements can trigger a wide range of chain reactions on the board, bringing great help to players. In addition, the game features a variety of purchasable and unlockable items, such as extra moves, bomb boosters, and obstacle removal tools, which players can use to break through difficult situations at critical moments. The skill system is determined by the character settings. Alice and other characters in Wonderland have unique abilities, such as eliminating blocks in specific areas, increasing the level time, or generating powerful special elements. By combining the skills of the characters, players will be able to dramatically increase their efficiency and experience a smoother gameplay.
One of the highlights of Alice Wonder Match is its colorful adventure mode. The game brings players into a fantasy world full of fairy tale colors, including the Mushroom Forest, the Red Heart Castle, the Mirror Lake and many other scenes, each scene is designed with a unique style and atmosphere. Players not only need to complete the three-canceling task, but also through the unlocking of new chapters and areas, and gradually repair the magic out of control and broken wonderland. Each time a level is completed, players will unlock a part of the storyline, which is presented with dynamic illustrations and text descriptions, vividly showing the adventures of Alice and her companions.
Character collection and development is another major feature of the game. Players can unlock more characters in Wonderland through level rewards, quest achievements and special events, which not only add more depth to the gameplay, but also enrich the presentation of the plot. Each character has a unique appearance, backstory, and skill attributes, and players can cultivate their characters to improve their levels and abilities to gain an advantage in more difficult levels. The development system also includes an equipment function that allows players to equip their characters with different accessories or props to further enhance their combat performance.
In order to extend the game's life cycle, Alice Wonder Match is designed with rich social and challenging content. Players can add friends to the game, compete with them in scoring, give each other stamina or props, and participate in multiplayer cooperative activities to unlock exclusive rewards. The game also includes a Race Mode and Leaderboards, which allow players to climb the global rankings and gain a sense of accomplishment by challenging difficult levels or completing hit missions. Additionally, limited-time events and seasonal updates add to the game's freshness, allowing players to participate in events to acquire limited characters, props, or scenarios that add to the game's playability.

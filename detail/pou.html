DESCRIPTION

Pou is a simulation and parenting mobile game crafted by Zakeh, which has won the hearts of many players around the world since its launch thanks to its simple yet fun gameplay and unique pet setting. The core of the game is to take care of an alien pet named Pou, which has a simple but very cute image, and players need to raise it in various ways to make it grow up healthy and happy. Despite its seeming simplicity, Pou's rich interactive elements, personalized settings, and varied mini-games have earned it a place in the mobile game market.
In Pou, players take on the role of Pou's owner and are tasked with taking care of the alien pet's daily life. resembling a brown, teardrop-shaped creature with two large eyes and a small mouth, Pou's expressions and movements are so infectious that players will quickly become attached to it despite its simplicity.
Pou's living environment is a simple and cozy home. Players can earn gold coins and complete missions to unlock new furniture, wallpaper, clothing and accessories to constantly decorate and upgrade Pou's home. There is no complex backstory or plot in the game, and the core experience focuses on interacting with and taking care of Pou.
The core gameplay is about taking care of Pou's daily needs and making sure it is healthy and happy. Players need to feed Pou regularly, clean its body, take care of its health, and even play with it. The game's interface is simple and intuitive, and is divided into several different rooms, each representing a specific type of interactive activity.
Kitchen: Here, the player can feed Pou a variety of food and drinks; Pou's dietary needs change over time, and the player needs to make sure it doesn't starve. In addition, Pou has its own preferences for different foods, and these preferences affect its mood and health status. During the feeding process, the player can purchase different types of food, some of which will increase Pou's weight and others will provide him with more energy.
Bathroom: Pou gets dirty over time and in the bathroom, the player can bathe it to keep it clean and healthy. To bathe, the player uses soap and water and cleans Pou's body by tapping and swiping the screen. After cleaning, Pou will show a happy expression and its mood will improve.
Bedroom: In the bedroom, Pou can rest and sleep. Players need to keep an eye on Pou's energy bar and put Pou to bed when it feels tired. pou's sleep time can be accelerated by using props that speed up the time or by waiting for Pou to naturally recover its energy.
Laboratory: The player can use medicines and supplements in the laboratory to take care of Pou's health. When Pou feels sick or is ill, the player can use specific drugs for it to heal it. The lab also provides some energy supplements that can help Pou regain its energy quickly.

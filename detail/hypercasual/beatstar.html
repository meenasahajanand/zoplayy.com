Beatstar is a rhythm game, which means that players need to tap or swipe on the screen in time with the music. The game uses a variety of different types of notes, including single notes, double notes, and hold notes. Players need to tap or swipe on the notes as they appear on the screen to earn points. Beatstar features a library of over 100 songs, from classic hits to modern chart-toppers. Players can unlock new songs by completing challenges, leveling up, or purchasing them with in-game currency.
Beatstar features a variety of challenges that players can complete to earn rewards. These challenges can range from simply playing a song to earning a certain number of points or stars.

In Mega Tower, players assume the role of a strategic commander tasked with safeguarding their land from hordes of invading enemies. To achieve this, players must strategically place an array of defensive structures, known as towers, along predetermined paths to thwart the advancing enemy forces. Each tower possesses distinct abilities and characteristics, ranging from archers and mages to artillery and traps.

As players progress through the game, they earn in-game currency or rewards that can be used to upgrade their towers. With each upgrade, players must make careful choices, balancing the enhancement of existing towers with the need to unlock new ones to effectively counter the evolving enemy threats.
Special Abilities and Power-Ups:
Mega Tower introduces an assortment of special abilities and power-ups that players can utilize strategically during battles. Goal of the Game:

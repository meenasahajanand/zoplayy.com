Editor's Review
Prepare yourself for a delightful and captivating gaming journey with Candy Crush Soda Saga! This delicious puzzle adventure introduces new aspects of enchanting gameplay, brimming with purple soda and excitement. Combine 3 candies to navigate through diverse game modes such as Soda, Frosting, Honeycomb, and Jam. With more than 10,000 delectable puzzles, new challenges await you at every turn. Play solo or join friends to vie for the top score. Stay tuned for monthly updates, exclusive quests, and thrilling match 3 puzzles. Get ready to satisfy your hunger for entertainment with Candy Crush Soda Saga!
How To Play?

Combine candies to unleash purple soda

Break the ice to progress in Frosting mode

Rescue the trapped Candy Bears by aligning candies near honeycomb

Spread the jam throughout the board in Jam mode

Uncover new matching combinations like Swedish Fish and Coloring Candy
